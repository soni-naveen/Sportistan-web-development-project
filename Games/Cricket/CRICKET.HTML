CRICKET



The 11th edition of Chitkara T-20 cricket tournament is being organized at the Chitkara University Punjab, for all Schools/Colleges. The tournament will start on 4th February 2018. The students interested to play can register their teams. Each team will consist of 15 players.

If a team cannot be formed from one year/school, then two or more years of the same school can form a single team but in this case a prior approval from concerned school is to be obtained and the same should be informed in written to Mr Harinder Pal Singh, Associate Director (University Sports Board). A team once formed will not be allowed to change its members.

2 ON 2 CRICKET TOURNAMENT ORGANIZED AT CHITKARA UNIVERSITY


11TH CHITKARA T20 – CRICKET TOURNAMENT

The final match was definitely a watcher’s delight as viewers came in huge numbers to cheer up for their team and motivate the players to play well. It was finally Rahul and Dhruv who managed to clinch the winning title. Apart from the winners, there were runners ups title bagged closely by Varun Jamwal and Himanshu Chajjta.

The tournament is a knockout i.e. a team that loses will be out of the tournament. Every team is required to register for the tournament. The team will only be added to the final database once they pay a total amount of Rs 3500/-. The last date for the registration of teams is 29th January 2018 (4 pm). Late fee for registration is Rs.500/- from 29th January until 1st February 2018.
